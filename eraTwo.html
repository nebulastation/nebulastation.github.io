CATERPILLAR GROW-TIME

I reimagined the classic Snake game as a caterpillar's growth journey, where each leaf eaten triggers a randomly selected bird out of 5 bird types to appear as an obstacle on screen. The object of the game is to eat and grow without being eaten. Each bird announces its presence with a species-specific bird sound.

Sound samples courtesy of Lang Elliott and Audubon.
App written in C++ / openFrameworks with support from the School for Poetic Computation.

CATALOG

I co-designed Radical Hope: the catalog with Aloe Corry and Madeline Rupard. The catalog and corresponding virtual exhibition feature work by 28 artists, playfully manipulated to draw out the essence of each artist and the collective. PDFs of the catalog were sent to donors giving $5+ to the show's GoFundme, with proceeds going to the International Rescue Committee's COVID-19 Relief Fund.

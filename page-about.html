ABOUT

Connie Fu (she/her) is an American-born daughter of Chinese immigrants. She is a transdisciplinary artist working in textiles, performance, and research-based practice to reconfigure traces of her bicultural heritage as living environments. Her work has been exhibited at Maelstrom Collaborative Arts (Cleveland, OH), FiveMyles (Brooklyn, NY), and La MaMa Theatre (New York, NY). She is the Gallery and Outreach Director at Praxis Fiber Workshop, an artist-run community fiber studio and educational center in Cleveland. These days, she is learning how to ferment soy sauce.

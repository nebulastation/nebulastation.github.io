ERA SYNTHESIS (2020 - )

In the Era Synthesis I am approaching the design and composition of sound and physical materials in a more personal, tactile, collaborative way.
This process-in-the-making isn't neat or well-defined, and draws on craft tradition and interpersonal relationships to hold timbrous connections between objects, ideas, and producers.

Click and drag panels to move them; click and release to go deeper.

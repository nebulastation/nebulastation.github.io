April 12, 2021

In March and April I facilitated Creative Work Group, a monthly session hosted by Maelstrom Collaborative Arts. In the two sessions the participants and I took on roles of IMAGE SUMMONER, SOUND GENERATOR, and MOTION FORGER and performed 5-minute scenes with improvised material. The goal of each session was to loosen up our creative faculties and remind ourselves of how unexpected connections can occur from seemingly disparate parts. More about Maelstrom.

World Unfound, my first EP, is out today.
It is a four-track experimental soundscape and the inaugural release under my dual moniker Nebula / Night King.

For a physical copy of the EP, ask me for a USB drive, which I will paint, enclose in a charming handwoven cloth pocket, and mail to you for sliding scale $8 to $20.
